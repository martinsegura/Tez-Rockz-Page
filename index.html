Tez Rockz

TezRocks is a chill NFT game about collecting rocks and stacking them. The rocks are traded on OBJKT.com in the Tezos blockchain. Game in development

How to play:

    1. Collect TezRockz on OBJKT.com
    2. Open the game on tezrockz.tez.page
    3. Insert your wallet address and click "Load Wallet"
